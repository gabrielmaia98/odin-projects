Caprese Salad Recipe

Description

Caprese salad (Italian: Insalata Caprese) is a simple, fresh Italian dish that celebrates the classic trio of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil. Traditionally arranged in alternating slices and drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of salt and pepper, it’s a light and refreshing salad often served as an appetizer or side.

It is all about quality ingredients and letting their natural flavors shine—perfect for any time you want something fresh and vibrant. Serve with a chilled white wine and enjoy in the sunshine or on a balmy summer evening!

This recipe text was adapted from this version.

Ingredients

  • 3 large heirloom tomatoes, sliced, or to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ½ cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil leaves on a serving plate, alternating between them.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!
